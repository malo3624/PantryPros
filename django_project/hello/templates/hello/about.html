Our Vision

To help college-age adults improve their cooking habits and live independently in a healthy, economical fashion.

What is Pantry Pros?

Pantry Pros is a web application that takes the food you have in your refridgerator or pantry and disply recipes of meals that can be made with the food you already own.

Features

  • Unique delicious recipes
  • Easy user interface
  • Grocery list
  • Short video demonstrating how to create the meal
  • Nutritional facts
